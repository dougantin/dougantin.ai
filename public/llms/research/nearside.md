# Nearside

Canonical URL: https://dougantin.ai/research/nearside

Nearside is a serialized field guide to life on the near side of cyberpunk. It showcases real-world tech and real news headlines through clearly marked fictional stories about ordinary life. The underlying claims are verified, sourced, and directly linked.

The editorial question is:

> What does a capable person carry, use, understand, and control in 2026?

The publication is practical, skeptical, technically literate, pro-agency, and slightly paranoid without becoming conspiratorial. The technology or headline is the evidence; the larger subject is the transition toward a more cybernetic, decentralized, automated, and biologically modifiable society.

## Issues

### Issue 003 — Everyday Carry Is a Threat Model

URL: https://dougantin.ai/research/nearside/003

A six-object pocket dump shows how a hardened phone, neural-control glasses, hardware keys, cellular-surveillance detection, telehealth sensors, and satellite messaging trade default systems for new dependencies.

Iteration: Moved from standalone vignettes to a connected loadout built around real-world tech that reads one person’s pocket as a lived threat model.

### Issue 002 — Everything Wants Proof
