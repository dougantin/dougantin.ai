## What It Is

John Severini's June 2026 essay examines how robotic warfare and dual-use AI move military leverage away from mass human mobilization and toward capital-intensive private infrastructure. The piece ties battlefield automation to sovereignty, legitimacy, and the state's changing dependence on citizens versus firms.

## When To Use It

Use it when thinking about autonomous weapons, defense software, state-capital power, the political cost of war, or how AI infrastructure changes who actually holds coercive capacity.
