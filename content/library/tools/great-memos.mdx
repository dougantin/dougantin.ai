## What It Is

Great Memos is a searchable archive of historic memos, letters, papers, and leadership notes from business and technology. It is most useful as a clean way to get to the original document quickly.

## When To Use It

Use it when you want the raw source material for strategy research, investment writing, historical references, or canon-building around influential documents.
