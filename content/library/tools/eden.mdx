## What It Is

Eden helps research high-performing posts across X, YouTube, Instagram, TikTok, LinkedIn, and Substack. It can organize swipe-file boards, analyze creator patterns, and turn those observations into outlines, scripts, and post variations.

## When To Use It

Use it for validated content ideas, hook and structure analysis, platform-specific writing, and repeatable content workflows where voice consistency matters.
