## What It Is

PostHog combines product analytics, session replay, feature flags, experiments, surveys, error tracking, and data pipelines into one stack. It is useful for teams that want product feedback and rollout control close to the engineering workflow.

## When To Use It

Use it when you need a practical product-instrumentation stack for shipping, measuring behavior, testing changes, and understanding where users or agents are getting stuck.
