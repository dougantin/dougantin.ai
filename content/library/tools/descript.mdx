## What It Is

Descript is a media editor where you edit audio and video by editing text. It also adds AI-assisted cleanup, clip generation, captions, voice tools, and workflow automation for conversational content.

## When To Use It

Use it when you want to move quickly on podcasts, screen demos, interviews, talking-head clips, or repurposed social content without a heavier post-production stack.
