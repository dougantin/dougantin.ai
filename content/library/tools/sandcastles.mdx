## What It Is

Sandcastles analyzes short-form video channels and clips, surfaces viral outliers, explains why they worked, and turns those patterns into hooks and scripts.

## When To Use It

Use it when trying to increase short-form video output with stronger research, better hooks, and a more repeatable ideation process.
