## What It Is

Screen Studio is a macOS screen recorder and editor with automatic zoom, smooth cursor animations, webcam and audio capture, subtitles, branded backgrounds, and quick exports or share links.

## When To Use It

Use it when a workflow, product walkthrough, tutorial, or launch clip needs to look polished quickly. It is especially relevant for public proof-of-work, product demos, and async explanations.
