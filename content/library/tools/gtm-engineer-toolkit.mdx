## What It Is

The GTM Engineer Toolkit packages reusable Claude Code skills for sales and marketing workflows: account research, lead qualification, personalized outreach, meeting prep, and weekly pipeline summaries.

## When To Use It

Use it as a reference or starting point for building repeatable AI-assisted GTM workflows around a specific product, ICP, voice, and sales process.
