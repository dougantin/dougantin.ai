## What It Is

Billion Dollar PDFs is a curated directory of high-leverage documents across technology, AI, crypto, venture, and broader market thinking. It adds lightweight framing around why each document mattered instead of acting as a raw archive alone.

## When To Use It

Use it when you want a thesis-oriented reading list of documents that shaped markets, narratives, or capital allocation and need more context than a plain PDF repository provides.
